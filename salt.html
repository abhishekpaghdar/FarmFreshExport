Salt

Product Description

  • Tata Salt Iodised Crystal Salt is sourced from the sea, is solar evaporated and hygienically packed.
  • It combines the goodness of iodine and crystal salt, and offers a natural taste. It is best used in dishes like sambhar, chutney and different types of pickles.
  • Tata Salt is made using a vacuum evaporation process that involves evaporating sea brine in steam-heated vacuum evaporators. This process produces a salt that is almost free of extraneous matter and has a fine crystalline structure.
  • Tata Salt contains iodine, which is an essential nutrient for humans that helps with mental development, thyroid function, and brain function.
