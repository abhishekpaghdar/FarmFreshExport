Mung Beans

Product Description

  • For the cultivation of moong, light loamy soil with good drainage is suitable. 2-3 Ploughings are recommended. Add 200 kg gypsum per acre of land at the time of tillage. For sowing of bold grained moong: 10 kg per acre in Zaid & 6-8 kg per acre in Kharif
  • Weed Control For weed control, one weeding must be done 20-25 days after sowing.For crop and soil development Apply organic matter at the right time.
  • If there is no rain for a long time at the time of growth, then irrigation is necessary for good production of the crop.Spraying substances such as lemon oil, jaggery and insecticides to kill pests and diseases. After that it will be convenient to weed again.
  • Green Mung can be harvested at 55 to 60 days after planting, yellow variety at 70-75 days . Fruits can be harvested once in 3 to 4 days.
  • Early morning hours are best suited for harvest. After harvest fruits should be kept in cool place and avoid direct exposure to sunlight.
  • Harvesting before the maturity of crop, usually result in lower yields, while delayed harvesting results in shattering of pods and other losses caused by pests. Harvesting during rains and overcast weather invitation to fungal infection.
